Mezhuku is a sustainable candle brand creating eco-friendly, recyclable, and toxin-free candles. Crafted from 100% pure soy wax and natural essential oils ethically sourced from the Western Ghats, we care for both people and the planet.

We partner with organizations to deliver meaningful corporate gifts that strengthen ESG (Environmental, Social & Governance) and CSR initiatives. Our gifts are sustainable, recyclable with zero-plastic packaging, authentic, and fully customizable to reflect your brand identity.
