Mezhuku is a Kerala-based lifestyle brand specializing in handcrafted candles and curated goods that celebrate the art of slow living. Our collections are inspired by nostalgia, comfort, and the quiet stories of everyday life. Whether it is a scent that evokes a cherished memory or an artistic piece for your space, every creation is crafted with meticulous attention to detail.

Founded by Punnya, the Mezhuku team is united by a commitment to authenticity, craftsmanship, and sustainable practices.

We believe in turning the everyday into moments worth remembering.

✨ Ignite Your Creativity: Join Our Workshops!

Ever wondered how to capture a memory in a scent? We host hands-on Candle-Making Workshops (available both online and offline) designed for beginners and enthusiasts alike. Dive into the art of slow living, learn the secrets of wax and fragrance blending, and craft your own magical creations.

To learn more or book your spot, drop us a message in the enquiry space below, or directly DM us on our Instagram page!
